What’s your typical turnaround for a colour grade?
Sahil M. · Filmmaker · 2 days ago
▶ Aakash Replies
Short films under 10 min: 24–48hrs. Features: 3–5 days. I always lock the edit first and deliver a grade reference for approval. Rush turnarounds available — just message me!
M
Do you work with Sony A7 LOG footage or only cinema cameras?
Maya K. · Videographer · 4 days ago
▶ Aakash Replies
Absolutely! Sony S-Log2/3, Canon C-Log, BRAW, ARRI LogC — all handled natively in DaVinci. A7 footage is one of my most common projects. I build custom 1D/3D LUTs per camera model for accurate transforms.

Can you do both edit AND colour grade in one package?
Rohan V. · Brand Director · 1 week ago
▶ Aakash Replies
Yes — this is my preferred workflow. Full pipeline: assembly cut → rough cut → sound design (Fairlight) → colour grade → motion graphics → final export. One timeline, zero handoff errors, always cohesive.

Standard packages include 2 revision rounds. If your brief is clear upfront, most projects rarely need more than 1. I use Frame.io for frame-accurate feedback, so revisions are fast and painless.
